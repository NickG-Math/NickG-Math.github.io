About

I am a 5th year PhD student in mathematics at the University of Chicago. My advisor is J. P. May.
I work in the field of algebraic topology. Part of my work involves developing computer software to assist with, and in many cases automate, the algebra involved in algebraic topology computations.
You can find my work in the links below.
